A Multi-Layered Technology Architecture

The system is built on a robust architecture that ensures high accuracy and delivers the right information to the right people at the right time.

Technology Stack Composition

Tiered Alert System

  • T1

    Tier 1: Internal Alert

    Instant, real-time alerts with location and visuals sent directly to TNFD staff and on-ground Elephant Response Teams (ERTs).

  • T2

    Tier 2: Targeted Public Alert

    Geofenced SMS and automated voice calls sent to registered residents within a specific radius of the elephant's location.

  • T3

    Tier 3: General Public Alert

    Remote activation of GSM-based alert lights in village centers, bus stops, and along key roads for broad visual warnings.

The Power of Prediction & Community
